The National Hockey League

The National Hockey League or NHL, is the highest level of professional hockey played in North America, with a total of 31 teams between Canada and the US. When the NHL ws first organized there were only 6 teams, now know as "The Original Six". Just this year (2017) the 31st team, the Golden Knights was added, located in Las Vegas Nevada.

The American Hockey League

The American Hockey league or AHL, is the second highest level of professional hockey played in North America. The AHL is considered the "minors" or "farm teams", because these teams are directly linked to a parenting NHL team, and players are frequently swapped "up" to their repsective NHL team and possibly back "down" to their AHL team. Most players on an AHL team hope to get "pulled up" to the NHL and remain there for the rest of their career.
